StegoCrypt Suite is designed with privacy as a fundamental principle. We believe your data belongs to you, and we've built our software to reflect that belief.

Data Collection

What We DON'T Collect

  • Personal files or documents processed by the Software
  • Encryption keys, passwords, or passphrases
  • Content of hidden messages or embedded data
